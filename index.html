About

Crafting objects that feel inevitable

Emmanuel de Mazieres is an industrial designer with over a decade of experience creating beautiful, well-crafted, and useful products. His work sits at the intersection of form, technology, and human behavior.

Currently Principal Industrial Designer, he has contributed to consumer electronics, wearables, and everyday objects — always with a focus on clarity, material honesty, and thoughtful interaction.

Previously he worked at IDEO, fuseproject, and other leading studios, developing a rigorous yet intuitive approach to design that balances strategic thinking with meticulous craft.

Based in San Francisco Bay Area
